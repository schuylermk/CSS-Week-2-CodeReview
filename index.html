Spella Caffè specializes in traditional espresso, macchiato and cappuccino- from the careful selection, roasting and blending of coffee beans to the hand-pulled extraction of the ristretto. Spella Caffè offers fresh-brewed, single-origin coffees; handcrafted and made-to-order chai and teas; Italian sodas; and gelato, sorbetto, and affogato.

Spella coffees may also be enjoyed at...

Alma Chocolate,Fire On The Mountain,The Fireside,FlavourSpot, Lion's Share Coffeehouse & Bakery,Market of Choice - Terwilliger,Nostrana, Raven and Rose,Sunny Day Coffee,and Tabor Bread
