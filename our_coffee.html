Our Coffees

Andrea Spella works closely with family-owned coffee farms and co-operatives in India, Latin America, and Africa to ensure the highest quality of beans. Strong relationships with coffee growers are the foundation of Spella Caffè. Andrea roasts the coffee beans in small batches―eleven pounds at a time―at his roastery in Southeast Portland. His roasting brings out the unique, inherent qualities of each variety. Spella Caffè is currently roasting the following varieties of whole bean coffees.

Signature Espresso Blend:

The lush textures of this classically Italian espresso carry chocolate and caramel tones―think crème brulée with candied orange and lime peel in the finish. Excellent for espresso, moka pot, and drip methods.

India Kattehollay:

Direct from the family and farm from Karnataka State, our exclusive Peaberry is light to medium bodied with exotic spices and a snappy finish. This coffee truly shines with drip methods.

Ethiopia Sidamo Gr. 2:

Vanilla, Nougat, with a Cabernet finish.

Ethiopia Chelfit Gr. 3:

Spiced Dutch Cocoa, Strawberries and a dry finish.

Rossalina:

An espresso blend named after our roaster! Thick, lush body, tons of crema, nutty, caramel tones with a healthy amount of licorice and anisette―versatile Rossalina is an afternoon pick-me-up or a wonderful end to a meal!

Guatemala "Tikal":
