Our Services

In addition to providing handcrafted coffees, and artisan-roasted beans, Spella Caffè works closely with a number of select wholesale customers. Andrea Spella has been assisting business owners in all facets of coffee and espresso sales for over a decade. Spella Caffè provides unparalleled customer service and support, including wholesale custom-roasted coffee beans; espresso and coffee brewing equipment including delivery, repair and maintenance; and comprehensive barista training. In addition, Spella Caffè provides coffee service for small and large gatherings and events, including meetings, lectures, fund-raisers and weddings.
